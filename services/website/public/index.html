The truth

"Every meeting tool tries to do everything — transcribe, summarize, integrate, automate. But busy people don't need more features. They need two answers: what are we here to do, and what do I need to do next? Everything else is noise."

Popouts gives you just those two answers. Nothing more, nothing less.

What it does

Agenda in. Notes down. Actions out.

That's the entire workflow. No learning curve, no setup, no 47-feature onboarding tour.

Set your agenda.

Before the meeting starts, write down what you want to achieve. Three meeting types — standup, one-on-one, and review — keep things structured without overthinking it.

Capture your notes.

During or after the meeting, jot down what happened. No templates, no formatting rules — just write. It's as fast as scribbling on a napkin, but you won't lose it.

Get your action items.

Popouts reads your notes and pulls out the action items automatically. Clear tasks, assigned owners, due dates — ready for you to knock out before the next meeting.

Why just two things

Meetings are simple. Your tool should be too.
