---

## Detailed Description

*Full store listing*

```
Popouts is a productivity extension that keeps your meeting prep and follow-up in one place.

Features:
• Meeting agendas — Add agenda items to meetings and keep them organized by category (1:1s, recurring, etc.)
• Notes — Capture notes during or after meetings
• Action items — Add actions manually or use AI to extract them from your notes
• Side panel — Access everything from a side panel without leaving your current tab
• Export & import — Backup and restore your data

Privacy:
Your data is stored locally in your browser. Notes are only sent to our API when you use AI action extraction. See our privacy policy for details.

Getting started:
Install the extension, open the popup or side panel, add your meetings, and start capturing notes and actions.
```

## Permission Justifications

| Permission | Justification |
|------------|---------------|
| **storage** | Stores meeting data, notes, agendas, and action items locally in the browser. |
| **sidePanel** | Provides a side panel for quick access to meetings and notes without leaving the current tab. |
| **host_permissions** (Railway APIs) | Used for AI action extraction from notes and for license validation. |
| **host_permissions** (popouts.app) | Used for the help page and other in-app links. |
| **host_permissions** (localhost) | Used only when developers switch to development mode in Settings. |
